About Me

My journey up to this point in my life has not gone the way I initially expected, as it goes for mostly everyone I imagine. My original plan to take my life after high school went a little like this:

  1. Go straight to college.
  2. Pick a major that sounds good where I could be successful coming out of College.
  3. Graduate and begin my career

As I mentioned earlier, it did not turn out how I planned. I actually ended up dropping out of College unfortunately and it all resulted from not having a plan and asking myself what I wanted to do for a career. Through my experience and just stumbling my way through life, I finally had an honest conversation with myself about what I wanted to do.

After taking some time and doing research, I found myself really interested in the tech space, particulary in Software Engineering. I found that bootcamps were becoming a viable option to enter the field and here I am now creating my first webpage through the Thinkful Program!

There are many reasons that peaked my interest in the field but the main ones are the following:

  • Leveraging Technology to create projects to ultimately help others in some way or another.
  • Working with others to create these types of projects really excites me!
  • Constantly educating yourself to help you excel in your career

One of my interests outside of Software Development is Fitness. I mentioned earlier that I had become a Certified Personal Trainer at the beginning of this year. Fitness has always been a constant in my life and always loved sharing knowledge and helping others live a healthier life in anyway, whether it be through nutrition or through their movement.
